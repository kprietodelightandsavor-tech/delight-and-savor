Curriculum made by a homeschool mama who lives the life.

I'm Kim — founder of Delight & Savor, Charlotte Mason educator, and the woman who teaches co-op literature while managing 46 animals on our 15-acre Texas ranch.

Every unit is tested in my own co-op class before it reaches your home. I built this curriculum because I couldn't find what I needed: materials that trusted my children with real ideas and taught them to read with attention.
